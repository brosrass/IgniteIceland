The Ignite Mission

At Ignite Iceland, our purpose is clear: we're here to bridge the gap for Swedish startups striving to secure their inaugural major client, propelling their growth. The realm of startups is rich with solutions for the myriad problems faced by both private and public sectors. Yet, the challenge often lies in making these crucial connections.

Ignite Iceland stands as a beacon for collaboration, a non-profit initiative dedicated to weaving a stronger network between the burgeoning startup ecosystem, seasoned corporations, and governmental bodies. Our goal is to foster meaningful commercial partnerships.

Our approach? By meticulously pairing startups with the ideal counterpart within organizations based on specific needs and hurdles, we pave the way for impactful first engagements. We also provide tailored support ahead of these pivotal meetings, ensuring discussions are fruitful and pave the way for lasting business relationships.

About Ignite Iceland

By creating a vibrant network of diverse partners, we open doors to new opportunities and transformative projects.
